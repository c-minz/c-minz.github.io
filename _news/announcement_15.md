We are hosting the [53rd Workshop on Foundations and Constructive Aspects of Quantum Field Theory](https://konferenz.uni-hannover.de/e/lqp53) at Leibniz University Hannover on 6-7 November 2026.

Abstract submission on topics of algebraic approach to QFT and axiomatic, mathematical, or constructive methods (Local Quantum Physics), as well as neighbouring fields, such as quantum information theory and quantum many-body systems are invited until 15. September.
Registration is open until 5. October.
